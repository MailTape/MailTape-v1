Our story

Overflowed with information, it's became very hard to find new talended artists. Of course there is Pandora and the like but with such a quantity of music out there now, it's easy to end up with a background music consumed like disposable tissues.

Being truly passionate about music, we wanted to find a way to help enthusiasts discover new music in an easy and consistent way.
That's why we created the MailTape. Every sunday morning, we send you an eclectic selection of heart-touching songs curated in collaboration with the most inspiring artists. You may not like everything you hear, but if you make at least one discovery then our job is done.

Created in 2011, more than 100 artists have been featured on MailTape, such as Nicolas Jaar, Connan Mockasin, Gonzalez, Wax Tailor, Shigeto. There are many more to come.
We hope you'll enjoy this experience. Now sit back, relax and listen...

  • Broken Bells - Holding On For Life

Energy is what makes the world moving! This morning, the german duo from Koln Vimes has decided to cheer us up with an amazing vibrant selection. Hope this will make you happy for, at least, the upcoming week.

Meet their sweet pop music

We all love pop music. But Vimes isn't just another random pop band. They somehow managed to capture something pretty unique. The progressive scheme of their tracks make them rapidly addictive and even if you keep on playing them for days, it's hard to get bored of the wonderful cool and positive wave which comes out of them.

Vimes' finest delicacies

Rhye - Open (James Manuel Faded Mix)

"Rhye was one of our this years favourites records. Jeff Samuels remix somehow manages to keep the originals tender intimacy and makes it a great dance-track at the same time."

Lost Scripts - I'll Be Watching You

"Lost Scripts is John Talabot and Pional, two amazing producers from Spain who teamed up for a release on Young Turks, a label we admire very much. Amazing track!"

Blood Orange - You're Not Good Enough

"We listened to the whole album (Cupid Deluxe) on the way to a show and this one's definitely one of its many highlights. Won't try and explain why so do listen to it and you'll see."

That's all folks! Time to enjoy the rest of your day. Many thanks to Vimes for this great episode. Amitiés.

Ludovic.
