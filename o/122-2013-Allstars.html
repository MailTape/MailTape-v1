Our story

Overflowed with information, it's became very hard to find new talended artists. Of course there is Pandora and the like but with such a quantity of music out there now, it's easy to end up with a background music consumed like disposable tissues.

Being truly passionate about music, we wanted to find a way to help enthusiasts discover new music in an easy and consistent way.
That's why we created the MailTape. Every sunday morning, we send you an eclectic selection of heart-touching songs curated in collaboration with the most inspiring artists. You may not like everything you hear, but if you make at least one discovery then our job is done.

Created in 2011, more than 100 artists have been featured on MailTape, such as Nicolas Jaar, Connan Mockasin, Gonzalez, Wax Tailor, Shigeto. There are many more to come.
We hope you'll enjoy this experience. Now sit back, relax and listen...

2013 is over. It's now time to head for a whole new adventure: 2014.

But first, let's have a step back and look at what has been accomplished. This year has been a important year for us. It has been the beginning of our new website. We hope you enjoy it. We also had the chance to collaborate with 31 awesome artists. Today, we've selected 7 of them.

We thought that you'd be happy to know what have they been up too lately. They are the ones who inspired us the most. So please, welcome our 2013 MailTape Allstars pick. If you'd like to get more, you can now access to our archives using the archives buttons which are on the top and on the bottom of this page.

2014 will be whatever you want it to be and we hope to be on your side to light up you sundays. Wether you'll experience joy or pain, we will remain still and do our best to please your ears with new musical experiences, inspiration and passion.

If you've appreciated our service, we would be grateful if you share the love all around you. You are our reason to be and we'd love to have more people on board. And there's a lot more to come. We're working on some new features we're unpatient to share with you soon.

Love and Best wishes for 2014.

Ludovic.
